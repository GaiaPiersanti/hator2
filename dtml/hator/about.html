Who Is Hator?


The name “Hator” is inspired by Hathor, the ancient Egyptian goddess of beauty, love, music, and joy. Revered as the patron of perfumers and artists, Hathor embodied elegance and sensory delight. At Hator Perfumes, we honor her legacy by crafting fragrances that celebrate beauty, passion, and creativity.
